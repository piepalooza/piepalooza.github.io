A pie experience like no other, the Paukstys Pie Palooza offers the largest buffet of pies you've ever seen.

Hand crafted by Natalie, a chef and cake designer.

In 2010 we decided to open our home to friends in our neighborhood to aleviate the dullness of the February & March months. What began as a small gather has grown to something we never expected. People from all walks of life gather in our home to experience Natalie's pies. Every pie is crafted from scratch to ensure the best flavor possible.

Though the party lasts for hours, the pie has been known to disapper in only a few. Last year alone 35 different pies were crafted for the event. Within 90 mins, every pie ws gone. We have people we've never met stop by because their friends used to live her and wish they could make it. We welcome all to our pie party. One thing's for sure, you won't regret coming.
